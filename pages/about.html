About

The New Gazette provides curated local news in an unbiased and balanced way, because news needs to be about helping people live well in their city, not about appeasing advertisers.

Part of our commitment to do right by our neighbors includes offering a sumamry of our most important headlines for free each month, in a format called The Monthly Brief. In an effort to ensure that these news summaries are maintained for the forseeable future they will be maintained on this website as pdf files, and made easily accessible on this site and via RSS.
