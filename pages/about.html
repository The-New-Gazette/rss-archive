About

The New Gazette provides curated local news in an unbiased and balanced way because news needs to help people live well in their city, not just appease advertisers.

Part of our commitment to do right by our neighbors includes offering a summary of our most important headlines from each month for free, in a format called The Monthly Brief. To ensure that these news summaries continue to be available, they are maintained on this website as pdf files and can be browsed on this site or via RSS.

Additionally, after our paid weekly editions are more than three months old, they will be made available here as well.

Happy reading!
